    Reproducible neuroimaging tools

    Tools

    • ReproNim - A Center for Reproducible Neuroimaging Computation (a good site to get up to date on doing reproducible neuroimaging research)

    • Code-ocean - A research collaboration platform that uses docker containers to let you run your analysis online

    • NeuroLinks - Resources for computational neuroscience

    The Brain Imaging Data Structure (BIDS)

    • The Brain Imaging Data Structure (BIDS) presented by Chris Gorgolewski within Neurohackademy 2018
      • duration: 56 min
      • type: [video]
    • BIDS website - A simple and intuitive way to organize and describe your neuroimaging and behavioral data
    • BIDS apps - Portable neuroimaging pipelines that understand BIDS datasets
    • BIDS starter kit - Collection of tutorials, wikis, and templates to get you started with creating BIDS compliant datasets
